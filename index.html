001 — Manifesto

Story is the original technology. The human operating system. Long before the slide deck, the website, or the brand guide, we were sitting around fires making sense of the world by telling each other what happened.

We pair the timeless principles of story with a strategic visual language — and we use that combination to give small businesses and nonprofits the kind of leverage usually reserved for companies a hundred times their size.

Two decades in, the work has only gotten clearer: tell the truth, tell it well, and make it impossible to ignore. We're here to help good people make a better world — and to make sure the world actually notices.
